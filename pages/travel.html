Getting There

Travel & Accommodation

Venue Address

Basemead Manor
Staploe, St Neots
PE19 5JB

By Car

The venue is easily accessible from the A1 and A428.
There is ample free parking available on site.

By Train

The nearest station is St Neots (approximately 10 minutes by taxi).
Services run regularly from London King's Cross.

Accommodation

For those wishing to stay nearby, we recommend:

The George Hotel, Buckden

A charming coaching inn just a short drive from the venue.

Premier Inn, St Neots

A reliable option with convenient access to the A1.

The Eaton Oak

Cosy rooms within minutes of Basemead Manor.
